What Guidera does

Walk anywhere. Learn everything. No planning required.

Tap any place

Tap a building, park, or street. In seconds, AI writes a rich, personalized description — history, architecture, hidden details.

Walkman mode

Pocket your phone, put earbuds in, and just walk. Guidera narrates each stop automatically as you approach it — no screen, no buttons.

Guided walking tours

Follow curated routes through the best of any city, or create your own AI-built custom tour. See the route, track progress, hear each stop.

Ask follow-ups

Curious about opening hours, the architect, or what happened here in 1842? Just ask. Every place becomes a conversation.

Live Activity & Watch

Track your tour from the Lock Screen, Dynamic Island, or Apple Watch. Current stop, distance, playback controls — all without opening the app.

Any city, any language

Works everywhere in the world. Choose your guide persona, preferred language, and how detailed you want descriptions to be.
